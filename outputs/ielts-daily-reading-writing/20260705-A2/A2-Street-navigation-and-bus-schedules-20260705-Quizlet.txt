planner (/ˈplæn.ər/) [noun]	A tool or person that helps arrange what to do - công cụ/người lập kế hoạch. Ví dụ: An online planner shows the next bus.
sign (/saɪn/) [noun]	A board with information or directions - biển báo. Ví dụ: The sign shows the bus route.
pavement (/ˈpeɪv.mənt/) [noun]	The hard path beside a road where people walk - vỉa hè. Ví dụ: Please wait on the pavement.
shelter (/ˈʃel.tər/) [noun]	A small covered place that protects people from rain or sun - mái che, nhà chờ. Ví dụ: The bus stop has a shelter.
ticket (/ˈtɪk.ɪt/) [noun]	A paper or digital pass that lets you travel - vé. Ví dụ: I bought a bus ticket.
press (/pres/) [verb]	To push something with your finger - nhấn. Ví dụ: Press the button before your stop.
leave (/liːv/) [verb]	To go away from a place - rời đi. Ví dụ: Leave through the side door.
safe (/seɪf/) [adj]	Not dangerous - an toàn. Ví dụ: Wait in a safe place.
calm (/kɑːm/) [adj]	Not worried or excited - bình tĩnh. Ví dụ: A calm rider checks the map again.
independent (/ˌɪn.dɪˈpen.dənt/) [adj]	Able to do things without much help - độc lập, tự lập. Ví dụ: Learning bus routes helps students feel independent.
bus stop (/ˈbʌs stɒp/) [noun phrase]	A place where buses stop for passengers - điểm dừng xe buýt. Ví dụ: The bus stop is near my school.
route (/ruːt/) [noun]	The way from one place to another - tuyến đường. Ví dụ: This route goes to the market.
destination (/ˌdes.tɪˈneɪ.ʃən/) [noun]	The place you are going to - điểm đến. Ví dụ: Type your destination into the app.
schedule (/ˈʃedʒ.uːl/) [noun]	A list of times when things happen - lịch trình, thời gian biểu. Ví dụ: Check the schedule before leaving.
direction (/daɪˈrek.ʃən/) [noun]	The way something moves or points - hướng. Ví dụ: Check the direction before you get on.
plan a trip (/plæn ə trɪp/) [verb phrase]	To prepare for a journey - lên kế hoạch cho chuyến đi. Ví dụ: We plan a trip before we leave home.
get on and off (/ɡet ɒn ənd ɒf/) [fixed expression]	To enter and leave a bus, train, or other vehicle - lên và xuống xe. Ví dụ: The planner tells us where to get on and off.
route number (/ruːt ˈnʌm.bər/) [noun phrase]	The number or letter that names a bus route - số tuyến. Ví dụ: Route number 12 stops here.
stop button (/stɒp ˈbʌt.ən/) [noun phrase]	A button riders press to ask the bus to stop - nút báo dừng. Ví dụ: Press the stop button before your stop.
traffic light (/ˈtræf.ɪk laɪt/) [noun phrase]	Red, yellow, and green lights that control traffic - đèn giao thông. Ví dụ: Cross the road at the traffic light.
