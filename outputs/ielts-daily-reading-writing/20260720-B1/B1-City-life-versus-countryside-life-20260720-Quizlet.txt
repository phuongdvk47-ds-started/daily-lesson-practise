urban (/ˈɜː.bən/) [adj]	Relating to towns and cities - thuộc về thành thị, đô thị. Ví dụ: Towns and cities are called urban areas.
suburban (/səˈbɜː.bən/) [adj]	Relating to an area on the edge of a city - thuộc về vùng ngoại ô. Ví dụ: Holywood is a suburban town of Belfast.
rural (/ˈrʊə.rəl/) [adj]	Relating to the countryside, not the city - thuộc về nông thôn. Ví dụ: Further away, you reach rural areas — the countryside.
countryside (/ˈkʌn.tri.saɪd/) [noun]	Land outside towns and cities, with fields and farms - vùng nông thôn, miền quê. Ví dụ: Many people enjoy the peace of the countryside.
hamlets (/ˈhæm.lət/) [noun]	Very small villages or settlements - những làng nhỏ (thôn xã rất nhỏ). Ví dụ: In the countryside you find villages and hamlets.
settlements (/ˈset.l.mənt/) [noun]	Places where people live - khu định cư. Ví dụ: Rural areas have smaller settlements than cities.
public transport (/ˈpʌb.lɪk ˈtræn.spɔːt/) [noun phrase]	Buses, trains, and other services for public travel - phương tiện giao thông công cộng. Ví dụ: Public transport is limited in the countryside.
open space (/ˈəʊ.pən speɪs/) [noun phrase]	Land that is not covered by buildings - khoảng không gian mở, đất trống. Ví dụ: The countryside has more open space than the city.
cleaner air (/ˈkliː.nər eər/) [noun phrase]	Air with less pollution - không khí trong lành hơn. Ví dụ: There is cleaner air in the countryside than in the city.
quiet environment (/ˈkwaɪ.ət ɪnˈvaɪ.rən.mənt/) [noun phrase]	A calm and peaceful place to be - môi trường yên tĩnh. Ví dụ: The quiet environment helps people feel relaxed.
live in the suburbs (/lɪv ɪn ðə ˈsʌb.ɜːbz/) [phrase]	To live in areas on the edge of a city - sống ở ngoại ô. Ví dụ: People who live in Holywood live in the suburbs.
nightlife (/ˈnaɪt.laɪf/) [noun]	Entertainment and activities at night - cuộc sống về đêm. Ví dụ: Cities offer plenty of cinemas, restaurants, and nightlife.
crowds (/kraʊdz/) [noun]	Large groups of people in a place - đám đông. Ví dụ: The noise, traffic, and crowds can increase stress in a city.
fast pace (/fɑːst peɪs/) [noun phrase]	A quick speed of activity and life - nhịp độ nhanh. Ví dụ: Some people find the fast pace of city life exhausting.
advantages and disadvantages (/ədˈvɑːn.tɪ.dʒɪz ənd dɪs.ədˈvɑːn.tɪ.dʒɪz/) [phrase]	The good points and the bad points of something - thuận lợi và khó khăn, ưu và nhược điểm. Ví dụ: Both ways of living have advantages and disadvantages.
distractions (/dɪˈstræk.ʃənz/) [noun]	Things that stop you from focusing - những thứ gây xao nhãng. Ví dụ: In a quiet village there are fewer distractions.
peaceful surroundings (/ˈpiːs.fəl səˈraʊn.dɪŋz/) [noun phrase]	A calm and quiet environment - môi trường yên bình. Ví dụ: Good concentration often improves in peaceful surroundings.
convenience (/kənˈviː.ni.əns/) [noun]	The quality of being easy and useful - sự tiện lợi. Ví dụ: Some people choose city life for its convenience and excitement.
rural areas (/ˈrʊə.rəl ˈeə.ri.əz/) [topic_vocabulary]	Countryside regions away from cities - các khu vực nông thôn. Ví dụ: There are not so many jobs in rural areas.
city life (/ˈsɪt.i laɪf/) [topic_vocabulary]	The way of living in a town or city - cuộc sống thành thị. Ví dụ: Cities offer more opportunities, but city life is not perfect.
