### Phần 1: Học từ vựng đơn giản (Simple Vocabulary)
| Từ vựng tiếng Anh | Nghĩa tiếng Việt |
| --- | --- |
| community | The people living in one particular area or people who are considered as a unit because of their common interests |
| diverse | Including many different types of people or things |
| background | A person's education, family, and experience of work |
| participant | A person who takes part in something |
| stimulation | An action or thing that excites or causes activity or development |
| dementia | A medical condition that affects the brain and memory, making it difficult to think clearly |
| neighborhood | The area of a town that surrounds someone's home, or the people who live in this area |
| planning | The act of deciding how to do something in the future |
| support | To help or encourage someone or something |
| prevent | To stop something from happening or someone from doing something |
| healthy | Strong and well; not sick |
| volunteering | The practice of doing volunteer work in a community |
| volunteerism | The principle of donating time and energy for community benefit |
| community project | A project designed to help a local community |
| volunteer work | Unpaid work done to help other people |
| cognitive function | The mental action or process of acquiring knowledge and understanding |
| executive function | Mental processes that enable us to plan, focus, and remember instructions |
| episodic memory | The memory of every day events that can be explicitly stated |
| social interaction | The process by which we act and react to those around us |
| cognitive decline | The gradual decrease in cognitive abilities, such as memory and thinking |
