I. Reading Passage
Why Should You Visit the Doctor?

Many people visit the doctor when they feel sick. A health appointment helps you talk to a doctor about your health problems. You can go to a health centre or a clinic near your home. Before the visit, you should register with the surgery. The first time you visit, the doctor will ask about your medical history. This helps the doctor understand your health condition better.

To make an appointment, you can call the surgery by phone, go online, or visit in person. The receptionist will help you find a good time. Some surgeries let you book on their website. You can choose a time that fits your schedule [R]. If you have an urgent problem, the surgery can give you a same-day appointment. The waiting room is where patients wait before seeing the doctor.

A physical examination helps the doctor check your health. The nurse may take your blood pressure. If you have symptoms like a cough or fever, the doctor will ask questions. The doctor will give you the right treatment or medicine. A pharmacist can also help with common illness like colds. Regular check-ups can improve [R] your health because the doctor finds problems early. It is important for everyone to attend [R] their appointments. You should never miss a hospital or clinic visit. Children need to see the doctor for vaccination. If you feel better after treatment, you can return to your normal activities. Remember, visiting the doctor is the best way to stay healthy.

Source: Adapted from NHS (National Health Service, United Kingdom) - "GP Appointments and Bookings" (2025-03-01)
URL: https://www.nhs.uk/nhs-services/gps/gp-appointments-and-bookings/
