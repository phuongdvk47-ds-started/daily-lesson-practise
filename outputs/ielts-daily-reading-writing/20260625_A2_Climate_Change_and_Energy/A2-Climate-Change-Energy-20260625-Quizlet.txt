renewable (/rɪˈnjuː.ə.bəl/) [adj]	Able to be replaced naturally by nature. - Có thể tái tạo.. Ví dụ: Wind and solar are renewable energy sources.
source (/sɔːs/) [n]	The place, person, or thing from which something comes or begins. - Nguồn, nguồn gốc.. Ví dụ: The sun is a powerful source of light and heat.
electricity (/iˌlekˈtrɪs.ə.ti/) [n]	A form of energy carried through wires for lighting and heating. - Điện, điện năng.. Ví dụ: We use solar panels to generate clean electricity.
drought (/draʊt/) [n]	A long period of dry weather with too little rainfall. - Hạn hán.. Ví dụ: The lack of rain caused a severe drought in the region.
condition (/kənˈdɪʃ.ən/) [n]	The state that something is in. - Điều kiện, trạng thái.. Ví dụ: Extreme weather conditions are becoming more frequent.
severe (/sɪˈvɪər/) [adj]	Very serious, bad, or extreme. - Khốc liệt, nghiêm trọng.. Ví dụ: Climate change leads to more severe storms and droughts.
efficient (/sɪˈvɪər/) [adj]	Working well without wasting energy, time, or money. - Hiệu quả, năng suất.. Ví dụ: Heat pumps are highly efficient technologies.
demand (/dɪˈmɑːnd/) [n, v]	A strong need or request for something. - Nhu cầu, yêu cầu.. Ví dụ: The demand for clean energy is increasing worldwide.
generation (/ˌdʒen.əˈreɪ.ʃən/) [n]	The production of energy or electricity. - Sự tạo ra, sự phát điện.. Ví dụ: Coal generation releases harmful carbon dioxide into the air.
predict (/prɪˈdɪkt/) [v]	To say what will happen in the future. - Dự báo, dự đoán.. Ví dụ: Scientists predict that global temperatures will rise.
responsible (/rɪˈspɒn.sə.bəl/) [adj]	Being the cause of something. - Chịu trách nhiệm, là nguyên nhân.. Ví dụ: Four countries were responsible for most of the new solar power.
decrease (/dɪˈkriːs/) [v, n]	To become less, or to make something less. - Giảm đi, sự sụt giảm.. Ví dụ: We must decrease the use of fossil fuels quickly.
solution (/səˈluː.ʃən/) [n]	A way of solving a problem. - Giải pháp.. Ví dụ: Renewable energy provides a great solution to climate change.
release (/rɪˈliːs/) [v]	To let gas or substance escape into the air. - Thải ra, giải phóng.. Ví dụ: Fossil fuels release greenhouse gases when burned.
increase (/ɪnˈkriːs/) [v, n]	To make or become larger in amount or size. - Tăng lên, sự gia tăng.. Ví dụ: The world's population needs an increase in clean energy.
record-breaking (/ˈrek.ɔːdˌbreɪ.kɪŋ/) [adj]	Better, bigger, or longer than any previous record. - Phá kỷ lục, vượt kỷ lục.. Ví dụ: 2023 was a record-breaking year for renewable energy.
clean-energy (/kliːn ˈen.ə.dʒi/) [n]	Energy that does not damage the environment or produce carbon. - Năng lượng sạch.. Ví dụ: Many solar farms supply clean-energy to local towns.
global-warming (/ˌɡləʊ.bəl ˈwɔː.mɪŋ/) [n]	A gradual increase in the overall temperature of the earth's atmosphere. - Sự nóng lên toàn cầu.. Ví dụ: Global-warming causes extreme droughts and hot weather.
wind-farm (/ˈwɪnd ˌfɑːm/) [n]	An area of land with a group of energy-producing wind turbines. - Trang trại điện gió.. Ví dụ: The new wind-farm was built near the coast.
fossil-fuel (/ˈfɒs.əl ˌfjʊəl/) [n]	A fuel like coal, oil, or gas, formed from old animal and plant remains. - Nhiên liệu hóa thạch.. Ví dụ: Fossil-fuel burning needs to decrease quickly to protect Earth.
make up for (/meɪk ʌp fɔː/) [phrasal verb]	To compensate for something lost or damaged. - Bù đắp cho, bù lại.. Ví dụ: Countries burned coal to make up for the loss of hydroelectric power.
in a row (/ɪn ə rəʊ/) [phrase]	One after another without a break. - Liên tiếp, liên tục.. Ví dụ: It was the 19th year in a row that solar was the fastest-growing source.
