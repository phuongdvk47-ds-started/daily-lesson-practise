### Phần 1: Học từ vựng đơn giản (Simple Vocabulary)
| Từ vựng tiếng Anh | Nghĩa tiếng Việt |
| --- | --- |
| planner | A tool or person that helps arrange what to do |
| sign | A board with information or directions |
| pavement | The hard path beside a road where people walk |
| shelter | A small covered place that protects people from rain or sun |
| ticket | A paper or digital pass that lets you travel |
| press | To push something with your finger |
| leave | To go away from a place |
| safe | Not dangerous |
| calm | Not worried or excited |
| independent | Able to do things without much help |
| bus stop | A place where buses stop for passengers |
| route | The way from one place to another |
| destination | The place you are going to |
| schedule | A list of times when things happen |
| direction | The way something moves or points |
| plan a trip | To prepare for a journey |
| get on and off | To enter and leave a bus, train, or other vehicle |
| route number | The number or letter that names a bus route |
| stop button | A button riders press to ask the bus to stop |
| traffic light | Red, yellow, and green lights that control traffic |
