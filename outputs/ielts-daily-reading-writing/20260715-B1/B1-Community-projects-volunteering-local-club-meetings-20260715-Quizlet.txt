cognitive function (/ˈkɒɡ.nə.tɪv ˈfʌŋk.ʃən/) [noun phrase]	The mental process of acquiring knowledge and understanding through thought, experience, and the senses - chức năng nhận thức. Ví dụ: Volunteering in late life is associated with better cognitive function.
executive function (/ɪɡˈzek.jə.tɪv ˈfʌŋk.ʃən/) [noun phrase]	Mental skills that include planning, focusing, remembering instructions, and juggling multiple tasks - chức năng điều hành (não bộ). Ví dụ: Executive function includes tasks like planning, focusing, and solving everyday problems.
episodic memory (/ˌep.ɪˈsɒd.ɪk ˈmem.ər.i/) [noun phrase]	The memory of every day events that can be explicitly stated or conjured - trí nhớ tình huống (trí nhớ về sự kiện diễn ra). Ví dụ: Verbal episodic memory is the ability to remember lists of words or events.
cognitive decline (/ˈkɒɡ.nə.tɪv dɪˈklaɪn/) [noun phrase]	A gradual decrease in mental abilities, such as memory and thinking speed - sự suy giảm nhận thức. Ví dụ: Volunteering in late life may protect the brain against cognitive decline.
dementia (/dɪˈmen.ʃə/) [noun]	A chronic or persistent disorder of the mental processes caused by brain disease or injury - chứng sa sút trí tuệ. Ví dụ: Regular activities can reduce the risk of Alzheimer's disease and related dementias.
baseline score (/ˈbeɪs.laɪn skɔːr/) [noun phrase]	A measurement that is taken at the beginning of an experiment or study for later comparison - điểm số cơ sở (điểm đo ban đầu). Ví dụ: Volunteering was associated with better baseline scores on tests.
diverse cohort (/daɪˈvɜːs ˈkəʊ.hɔːt/) [noun phrase]	A group of people sharing a common characteristic who come from varied backgrounds - nhóm nghiên cứu đa dạng. Ví dụ: They analyzed results from a diverse cohort of 2,476 older adults.
low-cost intervention (/ləʊ kɒst ˌɪn.təˈven.ʃən/) [noun phrase]	A cheap action or treatment taken to improve a medical or social situation - biện pháp can thiệp chi phí thấp. Ví dụ: Volunteering could serve as a simple, low-cost intervention in older adults.
cognitive stimulation (/ˈkɒɡ.nə.tɪv ˌstɪm.jəˈleɪ.ʃən/) [noun phrase]	Activities that challenge and exercise the brain, helping to keep it active - kích thích nhận thức. Ví dụ: Volunteering may protect the brain by offering cognitive stimulation.
social interaction (/ˈsəʊ.ʃəl ˌɪn.tərˈæk.ʃən/) [noun phrase]	The process of reciprocal influence between individuals in a community - tương tác xã hội. Ví dụ: Social interaction is essential for healthy cognitive aging.
physical activity (/ˈfɪz.ɪ.kəl ækˈtɪv.ə.ti/) [noun phrase]	Bodily movement produced by skeletal muscles that requires energy expenditure - hoạt động thể chất. Ví dụ: Volunteering combines physical activity and mental exercises.
community participation (/kəˈmjuː.nə.ti pɑːˌtɪs.ɪˈpeɪ.ʃən/) [noun phrase]	The engagement of individuals in activities that benefit their local society - sự tham gia cộng đồng. Ví dụ: Those who volunteered highlighted the value of regular community participation.
socioeconomic factors (/ˌsəʊ.si.əʊ.ek.əˈnɒm.ɪk ˈfæk.tərz/) [noun phrase]	Social and economic characteristics that influence an individual's life outcomes - các yếu tố kinh tế xã hội. Ví dụ: Some communities are more vulnerable to health issues due to socioeconomic factors.
statistical significance (/stəˈtɪs.tɪ.kəl sɪɡˈnɪf.ɪ.kəns/) [noun phrase]	A mathematical measure showing that an experimental result is unlikely to have occurred by chance - ý nghĩa thống kê. Ví dụ: The positive trend in cognitive retention did not reach statistical significance.
natural defense (/ˈnætʃ.ər.əl dɪˈfens/) [noun phrase]	An organic or inherent protection against disease or harm - hàng rào bảo vệ tự nhiên. Ví dụ: Active community involvement acts as a natural defense for the aging brain.
protective benefits (/prəˈtek.tɪv ˈben.ɪ.fɪts/) [noun phrase]	Advantages that guard against danger, damage, or decline - lợi ích bảo vệ. Ví dụ: Early study findings suggest that volunteer programs offer key protective benefits.
disparities (/dɪˈspær.ə.tiz/) [noun]	A great difference, especially one that is unfair or unequal - sự chênh lệch, sự khác biệt. Ví dụ: Promoting inclusive volunteer opportunities can address disparities in health outcomes.
epidemiology (/ˌep.ɪ.diː.miˈɒl.ə.dʒi/) [noun]	The branch of medicine which deals with the incidence, distribution, and control of diseases - dịch tễ học. Ví dụ: Rachel Whitmer is a professor of epidemiology at UC Davis.
volunteerism (/ˌvɒl.ənˈtɪə.rɪ.zəm/) [noun]	The use or involvement of volunteer labor, especially in community services - tinh thần tình nguyện, hoạt động tình nguyện. Ví dụ: Our findings show that volunteerism is important for cognitive aging.
brain health (/breɪn helθ/) [noun phrase]	The state of optimal cognitive, sensory, and motor function of the brain - sức khỏe não bộ. Ví dụ: Previous research on brain health has focused mainly on white populations.
