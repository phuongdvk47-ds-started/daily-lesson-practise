III. Writing Guidance / Suggested Answers
Task 1: Word Ordering (Sắp xếp từ thành câu hoàn chỉnh)
  • Seat belts keep us safe in cars.
  • Teacher's Guidance: Let's build this sentence together. We start with the plural Subject "Seat belts". Next, we need the active Verb "keep". What do they keep? "us" (object). In what state? "safe" (adjective). Where? "in cars" (prepositional phrase). Put them together: Seat belts keep us safe in cars.
  • Self-Correction Tip: Make sure you capitalized "Seat" and placed a period . at the end. Check that "keep" has no 's' since "belts" is plural.
Task 2: Sentence Completion (Hoàn thành câu)
  • 1. I always wear a seat belt when I sit in a car.
  • Teacher's Guidance: The prompt starts with "I always wear a seat belt when". The word "when" connects two clauses, so you need to add a full clause (Subject + Verb + Object) showing the time or situation. For example, I (subject) + sit (verb) + in a car (prepositional phrase). This gives: I always wear a seat belt when I sit in a car.
  • Self-Correction Tip: Check that your added clause has a subject "I" and a verb "sit".
  • 2. Car safety is important because it saves many people's lives in accidents.
  • Teacher's Guidance: Starting with "Car safety is important because", you need to add a full clause stating the reason. For example: it (pronoun subject) + saves (verb with 's') + many people's lives in accidents (object). This gives: Car safety is important because it saves many people's lives in accidents.
  • Self-Correction Tip: Check that "saves" is singular to agree with "it", and "people's" uses an apostrophe for possession.
Task 3: Translation (Dịch câu)
  • 1. The Swedish engineer invented the three-point seat belt.
  • Teacher's Guidance: Let's translate: "Kỹ sư người Thụy Điển" is "The Swedish engineer" (Swedish must be capitalized). "đã phát minh ra" is "invented" (past tense). "đai an toàn 3 điểm" is "the three-point seat belt". Put it together: The Swedish engineer invented the three-point seat belt.
  • Self-Correction Tip: Ensure you spelled "engineer" and "Swedish" correctly, and used the past tense "invented".
  • 2. Seat belts help protect drivers from serious injuries.
  • Teacher's Guidance: Let's translate: "Đai an toàn" is "Seat belts" (plural subject). "giúp bảo vệ" is "help protect" (help + base verb). "tài xế" is "drivers" (plural). "khỏi chấn thương nghiêm trọng" is "from serious injuries" (adjective "serious" before noun "injuries"). Put it together: Seat belts help protect drivers from serious injuries.
  • Self-Correction Tip: Verify the spelling of "injuries" (plural of injury) and "serious".
Task 4: Short Description (Mô tả ngắn)

Suggested Model Answer:

"Nils Bohlin invented the three-point seat belt in 1959. Its purpose is to protect drivers in car accidents. This safety invention has saved millions of lives." (26 words)
  • Teacher's Guidance: Let's learn how to write a description of an invention using facts! We can structure it into three sentences:

1. Sentence 1 (Inventor & Year): Tell who invented it and when (use past simple). (Nils Bohlin invented the three-point seat belt in 1959.)

2. Sentence 2 (Purpose): Explain what it is for. (Its purpose is to protect drivers in car accidents.)

3. Sentence 3 (Impact): Describe its long-term historical impact (use present perfect). (This safety invention has saved millions of lives.)

This three-sentence outline is perfect for describing inventions in IELTS!

  • Self-Correction Tip: Use "in 1959" (preposition "in" for years) and "has saved" (present perfect for ongoing impact).
Task 5: (*) Guided Email Invite (Viết email ngắn)

Suggested Model Answer:

"Hi Phong,
Would you like to go to the science exhibition with me next Saturday? We can see many famous inventions there. We can meet at the museum at 10:00 AM.
Write back soon!
Best,
Hoa" (39 words)
  • Teacher's Guidance: Let's write an email invitation using our friendly checklist:

1. Greeting: Friendly opening (Hi Phong,).

2. The Invite: Invite them politely (Would you like to go to the science exhibition with me next Saturday?).

3. Detail/Reason: What you will do there (We can see many famous inventions there.).

4. Meeting details: Time and place (We can meet at the museum at 10:00 AM.).

5. Sign-off: Close warmly (Best, followed by your name).

  • Self-Correction Tip: Use "on Saturday" or "next Saturday" and "at 10:00 AM". Capitalize "Saturday" and the recipient's name "Phong".
IV. Review Bridge / Ôn tập

Hãy thử thực hiện bài tập nhỏ sau để ôn tập từ vựng từ chủ đề Public Facilities của ngày hôm trước:

1. Sửa lỗi sai trong câu: "The museum is twice as larger as the local library." (Đáp án: sửa "larger" thành "large")
2. Viết lại câu bằng cách dùng mệnh đề quan hệ để nối: "This is the central library. I usually borrow books from this place." (Ví dụ: This is the central library where I usually borrow books / from which I usually borrow books.)
