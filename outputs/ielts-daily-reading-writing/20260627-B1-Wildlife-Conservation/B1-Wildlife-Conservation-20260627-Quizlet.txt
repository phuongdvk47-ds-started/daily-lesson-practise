threat (/θret/) [n]	A sign of danger - Mối đe dọa. Ví dụ: Water scarcity is now the biggest threat to wildlife.

ranger (/ˈrei̯ndʒər/) [n]	A person protecting a park - Nhân viên kiểm lâm. Ví dụ: Rangers patrol the forest daily to check on the animals.
journey (/ˈdʒɜːrni/) [n]	Traveling from one place to another - Hành trình, chuyến đi. Ví dụ: The trucks started a long journey of 700 kilometers.

spokesperson (/ˈspoʊkspɜːrsn/) [n]	A person speaking for a group - Người phát ngôn. Ví dụ: The spokesperson explained the details of the project.

conflict (/ˈkɑːnflɪkt/) [n/v]	Disagreement or fight - Xung đột, sự xung đột. Ví dụ: There is a constant conflict between humans and elephants.
