### Phần 1: Học từ vựng đơn giản (Simple Vocabulary)
| Từ vựng tiếng Anh | Nghĩa tiếng Việt |
| --- | --- |
| extreme | Very severe or unusual; much more than average |
| record | Higher, faster, or greater than ever achieved before |
| climatologist | A scientist who studies climate patterns and climate change |
| disaster | A sudden accident or natural event that causes great damage or loss of life |
| suffering | Physical or mental pain that someone experiences |
| pace | The speed at which something happens or moves |
| average | Normal or typical; calculated by adding amounts and dividing by the count |
| pre-industrial | Relating to the time before factories and modern industry were developed |
| influence | The power to affect how someone or something develops or behaves |
| humidity | The amount of water vapor in the air |
| vapor | A gas-like state of water or other liquids suspended in the air |
| generate | To produce or create something, like energy, rain, or electricity |
| prediction | A statement about what you think will happen in the future |
| flood | To become filled or covered with water; a large amount of water covering land |
| severe | Very serious, harsh, or extreme |
| frequent | Happening often or repeatedly |
| temperature | The measurement of how hot or cold something is |
| drought | A long period of time when there is little or no rain |
| threaten | To be likely to cause harm or damage to someone or something |
| consequence | A result or effect of an action or condition, usually negative |
| play a role in [R] | To be involved in or contribute to something |
| vulnerable [R] | At risk of harm or easily affected by something negative |
| in the long run [R] | Over a long period of time; eventually |
