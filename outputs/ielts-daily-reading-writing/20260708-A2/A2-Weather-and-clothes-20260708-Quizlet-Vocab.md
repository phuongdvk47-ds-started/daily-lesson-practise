| lightweight | Weighing only a little or less than average |
| choice | An act of choosing between two or more possibilities |
| comfortable | Producing a relaxing feeling |
| climate | The general weather conditions usually found in a particular place |
| thin | Having a small distance between two opposite sides |
| cotton | A soft thread or cloth made from a plant |
| protect | To keep someone or something safe from injury or damage |
| thick | Having a large distance between two sides |
| gloves | Pieces of clothing that cover your fingers and hands |
| environment | The air, water, and land in or on which people, animals, and plants live |
| electricity | A form of energy that provides power |
