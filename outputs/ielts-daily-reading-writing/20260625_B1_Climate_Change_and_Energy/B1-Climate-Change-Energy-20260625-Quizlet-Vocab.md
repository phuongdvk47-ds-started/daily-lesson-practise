| pre-industrial | /ˌpriː.ɪnˈdʌs.tri.əl/ | adj | Existing or occurring before the development of modern industry. - Trước thời kỳ công nghiệp. | We compare modern temperatures with the pre-industrial average. |
| greenhouse gas | /ˈɡriːn.haʊs ˌɡæs/ | n | A gas in the atmosphere that traps heat, contributing to global warming. - Khí nhà kính. | Carbon dioxide is a major greenhouse gas produced by factories. |
