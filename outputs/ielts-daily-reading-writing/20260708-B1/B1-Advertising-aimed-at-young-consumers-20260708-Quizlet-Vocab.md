### Phần 1: Học từ vựng đơn giản (Simple Vocabulary)
| Từ vựng tiếng Anh | Nghĩa tiếng Việt |
| --- | --- |
| advertiser | A person or company that advertises a product or service |
| persuasive | Good at making someone do or believe something through reasoning |
| targeted | Directed at a particular group or activity |
| celebrity endorsement | The use of a famous person to advertise a product |
| inadequacy | The state of not being good enough or confident enough |
| obesity | The condition of being extremely fat or overweight in a way that is dangerous for health |
| media literacy | The ability to critically analyze and evaluate media messages |
| manipulate | To control something or someone to your advantage, often unfairly |
| regulate | To control something, especially by making it work in a particular way |
| prohibited | Forbidden by law, rule, or other authority |
| commercial motive | A reason for doing something that is related to making a profit |
| audience | The group of people together in one place to watch or listen to a play, film, someone speaking, etc. |
| stealth marketing | The practice of promoting products to people without them knowing they are being marketed to |
| free trade | International buying and selling of goods, without limits on the amount of goods that one country can sell to another |
| enforce | To make people obey a law, or to make a particular situation happen or be accepted |
| purchasing decision | The thought process that leads a consumer from identifying a need to acting on it |
| billboard | A very large board on which advertisements are shown |
| customized | Made or changed according to the buyer's or user's needs |
| cognitive maturity | The ability to think, learn, and remember at an adult level |
| skepticism | An attitude of doubting that claims or statements are true |
