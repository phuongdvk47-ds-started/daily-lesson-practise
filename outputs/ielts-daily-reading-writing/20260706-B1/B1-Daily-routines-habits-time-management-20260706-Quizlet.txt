persist (/pəˈsɪst/) [verb]	To continue to exist or happen - kéo dài, dai dẳng. Ví dụ: His groggy feeling can persist for hours.
focus (/ˈfəʊ.kəs/) [verb/noun]	To give all your attention to something - tập trung. Ví dụ: It is hard to focus when you are tired.
melodic (/məˈlɒd.ɪk/) [adjective]	Having a pleasant tune or melody - du dương, có giai điệu. Ví dụ: A melodic alarm helps you wake up fresh.
tune (/tʃuːn/) [noun]	A series of musical notes that are pleasant to listen to - giai điệu, điệu nhạc. Ví dụ: She hummed a happy tune.
melody (/ˈmel.ə.di/) [noun]	A sequence of notes that make up a tune - giai điệu. Ví dụ: This song has a beautiful melody.
attention (/əˈten.ʃən/) [noun]	Notice, thought, or consideration - sự chú ý. Ví dụ: The speaker got the audience's attention.
