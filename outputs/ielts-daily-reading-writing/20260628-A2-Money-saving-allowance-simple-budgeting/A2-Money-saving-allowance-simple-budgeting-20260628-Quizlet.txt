value (/ˈvæljuː/) [n]	The importance or worth of something - Giá trị. Ví dụ: Children should learn the value of saving money early.

expert (/ˈekspɜːt/) [n]	A person with special knowledge or skill in a subject - Chuyên gia. Ví dụ: Financial experts suggest opening a savings account for kids.
encourage (/ɪnˈkʌrɪdʒ/) [v]	To make someone more likely to do something - Khuyến khích, động viên. Ví dụ: Teachers encourage students to save their pocket money.
savings account (/ˈseɪvɪŋz əˈkaʊnt/) [n]	A bank account where you keep money to save - Tài khoản tiết kiệm. Ví dụ: I put five dollars into my savings account every week.
grow (/ɡrəʊ/) [v]	To increase in size, amount, or value - Phát triển, sinh sôi. Ví dụ: Over time, the money in a savings account will grow.
household (/ˈhaʊshəʊld/) [n/adj]	Relating to a house and the people living in it - Hộ gia đình, thuộc gia đình. Ví dụ: Cleaning the living room is a regular household chore.
operate (/ˈɒpəreɪt/) [v]	To work or run a business or machine - Hoạt động, vận hành. Ví dụ: Tying allowances to extra chores shows kids how businesses operate.
expensive (/ɪkˈspensɪv/) [adj]	Costing a lot of money - Đắt đỏ. Ví dụ: Bicycles are too expensive to buy in one week.
sharing (/ˈʃeərɪŋ/) [n/v]	Giving a part of something to others - Sự chia sẻ, chia sẻ. Ví dụ: Sharing money with charity teaches children kindness.
immediately (/ɪˈmiːdiətli/) [adv]	Now or without waiting - Ngay lập tức. Ví dụ: Don't spend all your allowance immediately after receiving it.
pocket money (/ˈpɒkɪt ˈmʌni/) [n]	A small amount of money given to children - Tiền tiêu vặt. Ví dụ: He spent all his pocket money on candy.
manage (/ˈmænɪdʒ/) [v]	To control or organize something, like money - Quản lý. Ví dụ: It is hard for young children to manage a monthly budget.
spend (/spend/) [v]	To give money to pay for goods or services - Chi tiêu. Ví dụ: I spend five dollars on food and save the rest.
save (/seɪ/) [v]	To keep money for future use - Tiết kiệm. Ví dụ: If you save your money, you can buy a new book.
afford (/əˈfɔːd/) [v]	To have enough money to buy something - Có đủ khả năng chi trả. Ví dụ: I cannot afford that expensive toy.
financial (/faɪˈnænʃl/) [adj]	Relating to money or how it is managed - Thuộc về tài chính. Ví dụ: Budgeting is a very important financial skill.
make choices (/meɪk tʃɔɪsɪz/) [v phrase]	To choose between different options - Đưa ra lựa chọn. Ví dụ: Children must learn to make choices when spending.
