### Phần 1: Học từ vựng đơn giản (Simple Vocabulary)
| Từ vựng tiếng Anh | Nghĩa tiếng Việt |
| --- | --- |
| renewable | Able to be replaced naturally by nature. |
| source | The place, person, or thing from which something comes or begins. |
| electricity | A form of energy carried through wires for lighting and heating. |
| drought | A long period of dry weather with too little rainfall. |
| condition | The state that something is in. |
| severe | Very serious, bad, or extreme. |
| efficient | Working well without wasting energy, time, or money. |
| demand | A strong need or request for something. |
| generation | The production of energy or electricity. |
| predict | To say what will happen in the future. |
| responsible | Being the cause of something. |
| decrease | To become less, or to make something less. |
| solution | A way of solving a problem. |
| release | To let gas or substance escape into the air. |
| increase | To make or become larger in amount or size. |
| record-breaking | Better, bigger, or longer than any previous record. |
| clean-energy | Energy that does not damage the environment or produce carbon. |
| global-warming | A gradual increase in the overall temperature of the earth's atmosphere. |
| wind-farm | An area of land with a group of energy-producing wind turbines. |
| fossil-fuel | A fuel like coal, oil, or gas, formed from old animal and plant remains. |
| make up for | To compensate for something lost or damaged. |
| in a row | One after another without a break. |
