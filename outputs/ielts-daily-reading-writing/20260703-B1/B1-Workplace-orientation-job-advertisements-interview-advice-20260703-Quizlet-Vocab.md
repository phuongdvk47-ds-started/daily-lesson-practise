### Phần 1: Học từ vựng đơn giản (Simple Vocabulary)
| Từ vựng tiếng Anh | Nghĩa tiếng Việt |
| --- | --- |
| interview | A meeting where someone is asked questions to see if they are suitable for a job |
| benefits | Things such as medical insurance that employees receive in addition to their pay |
| recruiter | A person whose job is to find new people to join a company |
| disrupt | To prevent something from continuing in its usual way |
| internship | A period of training spent by a student or graduate in an organization |
| absolutely | Completely; used for emphasis |
| frustrated | Feeling annoyed or less confident because you cannot achieve what you want |
| prioritize | To decide which tasks are the most important so you can do them first |
| executive | Someone in a high position who makes decisions in a business |
| dismiss | To officially end the employment of a worker; to fire |
| etiquette | Rules of polite behavior in society or in a business |
| overqualified | Having more skills or experience than is needed for a job |
| respondent | A person who answers questions in a survey or test |
| supervisor | A person who is in charge of a group of people or an area of work |
| extension | An extra period of time given to complete a task |
| soft skills | Personal qualities that help you interact effectively with others |
| etiquette | Nghi thức, phép xã giao |
