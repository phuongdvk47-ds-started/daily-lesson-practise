endangered (/ɪnˈdeɪn.dʒəd/) [adj]	Animals or plants that may soon not exist. - Có nguy cơ tuyệt chủng, gặp nguy hiểm.. Ví dụ: The Iberian lynx is one of the most endangered cats in the world.
extinct (/ɪkˈstɪŋkt/) [adj]	No longer existing. - Tuyệt chủng, đã biến mất hoàn toàn.. Ví dụ: Many species of dinosaurs became extinct millions of years ago.
habitat (/ˈhæb.ɪ.tæt/) [n]	The natural home of an animal or plant. - Môi trường sống tự nhiên.. Ví dụ: Human development destroys the natural habitat of wild animals.
protect (/prəˈtekt/) [v]	To keep someone or something safe. - Bảo vệ.. Ví dụ: We must protect forests to save wild animals from extinction.
species (/ˈspiː.ʃiːz/) [n]	A set of animals or plants in which the members have similar characteristics. - Loài, các loài.. Ví dụ: There are many species of wild cats living in the forests.
recovery (/rɪˈkʌv.ər.i/) [n]	The process of returning to a normal or healthy state. - Sự phục hồi, khôi phục.. Ví dụ: The recovery of the Iberian lynx population is a great success.
wildlife (/ˈwaɪld.laɪf/) [n]	Animals, birds, and plants that live in natural conditions. - Động vật hoang dã.. Ví dụ: National parks are great places to watch native wildlife.
breed (/briːd/) [v]	To produce young animals. - Sinh sản, nhân giống.. Ví dụ: Some animals do not breed easily when they are in captivity.
population (/ˌpɒp.jəˈleɪ.ʃən/) [n]	All the organisms of the same group or species in an area. - Quần thể, số lượng cá thể.. Ví dụ: The wild rabbit population decreased quickly due to a new disease.
prey (/preɪ/) [n]	An animal that is hunted and killed by another for food. - Con mồi.. Ví dụ: Rabbits are the main prey of the Iberian lynx.
threat (/θret/) [n]	Something that is likely to cause danger or harm. - Mối đe dọa.. Ví dụ: Climate change is a major threat to global biodiversity.
successful (/səkˈses.fəl/) [adj]	Achieving the results that you wanted. - Thành công.. Ví dụ: The captive breeding program was very successful.
decrease (/dɪˈkriːs/) [v]	To become less, or to make something less. - Giảm đi.. Ví dụ: The number of wild tigers continues to decrease in Asia.
increase (/ɪnˈkriːs/) [v]	To become larger or greater in amount. - Tăng lên.. Ví dụ: We hope the tiger population will increase in the future.
prevent (/prɪˈvent/) [v]	To stop something from happening. - Ngăn chặn, ngăn ngừa.. Ví dụ: We must protect the forests to prevent soil erosion.
preserve (/prɪˈzɜːv/) [v]	To keep something safe or in its original state. - Bảo tồn, giữ gìn.. Ví dụ: We should preserve natural forests for wild animals.
decline (/dɪˈklaɪn/) [n, v]	A continuous decrease in number or quality. - Sự suy giảm, giảm sút.. Ví dụ: The decline in rabbit numbers made it hard for the lynx to find food.
captive (/ˈkæp.tɪv/) [adj]	Kept in a space and not free to leave. - Bị giam cầm, nuôi nhốt.. Ví dụ: Captive lynx are raised in centers before being released.
release (/rɪˈliːs/) [v]	To allow an animal to run free in the wild. - Thả, phóng thích, giải phóng.. Ví dụ: Conservationists release captive-bred lynx back into the forest.
survive (/səˈvaɪv/) [v]	To continue to live or exist after danger. - Sống sót, sinh tồn.. Ví dụ: Without rabbits to eat, many young cats could not survive.
wildlife-conservation (/ˈwaɪld.laɪf ˌkɒn.səˈveɪ.ʃən/) [n]	The practice of protecting wild species and their habitats. - Bảo tồn động vật hoang dã.. Ví dụ: She wants to study wildlife-conservation at university.
well-known (/wel nəʊn/) [adj]	Known widely or thoroughly; famous. - Nổi tiếng, được biết đến rộng rãi.. Ví dụ: The Iberian lynx is a well-known symbol of Spain's wildlife.
plant-eating (/plɑːnt ˈiː.tɪŋ/) [adj]	Feeding on plants (herbivorous). - Ăn thực vật.. Ví dụ: Rabbits are plant-eating animals that eat grass and leaves.
captive-bred (/ˈkæp.tɪv bred/) [adj]	Animals born and raised in controlled environments. - Được nuôi sinh sản trong chuồng trại/trung tâm bảo tồn.. Ví dụ: Captive-bred animals are eventually returned to their wild homes.
grassland (/ˈɡrɑːs.lænd/) [n]	Large open area of country covered with grass. - Đồng cỏ, thảo nguyên.. Ví dụ: Many wild animals live in grasslands because they can find lots of food.
die out (/daɪ aʊt/) [phrasal verb]	To become less common and finally stop existing completely. - Tuyệt chủng, chết dần hết, biến mất hoàn toàn.. Ví dụ: Dinosaur species died out millions of years ago.
run out of (/rʌn aʊt ɒv/) [phrase]	To use all of something and have no more left. - Cạn kiệt, hết sạch cái gì đó.. Ví dụ: If we run out of fresh water, we will face a major survival crisis.
