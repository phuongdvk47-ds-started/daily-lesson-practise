I. Reading Passage
Successful Student Morning Routines and Time Management

Having a structured morning routine is essential for a successful student lifestyle [R]. When students start their day early and plan their activities, they feel more in control. A good habit is to review the daily schedule before school begins. This planning reduces stress and helps them focus on their lessons throughout the day.

In addition to planning, daily physical activity and healthy sleep patterns are very important. Many students have to commute [R] to school by bus or bicycle. If they include a short walk or stretch in their morning routine, they feel more alert and ready to learn. Adequate sleep also improves their working memory [R] and cognitive [R] development, which are needed for solving complex math and science problems.

Finally, using school facilities [R] effectively plays a major role in time management. Successful students often spend their break times in quiet study areas or libraries. These public facilities [R] allow them to complete homework without distraction. In contrast, studying in noisy environments reduces focus. Overall, a balanced daily routine helps students keep their study schedule on track.
