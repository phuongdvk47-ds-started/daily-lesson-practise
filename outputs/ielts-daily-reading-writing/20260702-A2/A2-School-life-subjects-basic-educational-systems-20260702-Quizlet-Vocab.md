### Phần 1: Học từ vựng đơn giản (Simple Vocabulary)
| Từ vựng tiếng Anh | Nghĩa tiếng Việt |
| --- | --- |
| traditional | Following or belonging to customs or ways of behaving that have continued in a group of people for a long time |
| calendar | A system for measuring time in hours, days, weeks, and months |
| society | A large group of people who live together in an organized way |
| industrial | Connected with industry or having many developed industries |
| critic | A person who expresses disapproval of something and points out mistakes |
| vacation | A period of time when you do not go to work or school and can travel or relax |
| forget | To be unable to remember a fact, something that happened, or how to do something |
| pressure | A difficult situation that makes you feel worried or unhappy because you have too much to do |
| improve | To make something better, or to become better |
| performance | How well a person does a piece of work or an activity |
| lengthen | To make something longer, or to become longer |
| costly | Expensive, costing a lot of money |
| employee | Someone who is paid to work for a person or organization |
| object | To feel or express opposition to or dislike of something |
| extend | To add to something in order to make it longer or bigger |
| attend | To go to an event, place, or public institution regularly |
| subject | An area of knowledge that is studied in school, college, or university |
| prepare | To make something or someone ready for something that will happen in the future |
| recommend | To suggest that someone or something is good, or to advise a course of action |
| nutritious | Containing many of the substances that help the body to grow and stay healthy |
| take notes | To write down important information during a lesson |
| pass an exam | To be successful in a test |
| school calendar | The yearly schedule of school days and holidays |
| make progress | To improve or move forward in something |
| schedule | A plan showing times for activities |
| benefit | An advantage or good result |
