I. READING PASSAGE
Leisure Activities and Sports Clubs
Having a hobby or joining a sports club is a great way to spend your free time. Many sports clubs are
available [R] in local neighborhoods, offering activities like swimming, soccer, or tennis. When you join a
club, you can meet new people who share your interests. It is important to be polite [R] to other members, as
this helps create a friendly environment for everyone.
Leisure activities can also help you reduce stress after a busy day. If you want to join a local team, you
can usually visit their website and make a request [R] online. Hobbies are different from work or study
because you choose them for pleasure. They give you a sense of achievement when you learn a new skill or
win a game.
Finally, hobbies make your life more interesting. People who have active hobbies often have many stories
to share with others. Whether you enjoy painting, playing a musical instrument, or playing chess, leisure time
is essential for your happiness. It gives you a healthy break from your daily routine.
