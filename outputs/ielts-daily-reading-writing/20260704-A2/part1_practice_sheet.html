I. Reading Passage
Family Celebrations and Household Routines

Every family has its own unique way of living. A healthy household needs good routines and special celebrations. In many homes, a daily routine helps everyone know what to do. For example, parents and children might share chores, like washing dishes or sweeping the floor. When family members work together, they can avoid arguments and keep the house clean. This habit [R] makes daily life much easier and less stressful.

However, life is not only about daily work. Special events, like birthdays or New Year's Eve, are also very important. During these times, relatives often travel from different places to gather together. They prepare delicious food, give gifts, and share happy memories. These celebrations help to build a strong bond between family members. Even a simple weekend dinner can make a big difference [R] if everyone sits together and talks about their week.

In the past, families usually lived close to each other. Today, many young people move to big cities for work or study. Because of this, it can be difficult to meet regularly. To stay connected, modern families often use video calls to chat online [R]. They also try to plan at least one big family trip every year. Keeping in touch shows that you care about your family, and it helps everyone feel better [R] when they face difficult challenges in life.

Source: Adapted from Psychology Today - "Family life and household dynamics" (2023-01-10)
