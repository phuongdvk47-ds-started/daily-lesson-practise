### Phần 1: Học từ vựng đơn giản (Simple Vocabulary)
| Từ vựng tiếng Anh | Nghĩa tiếng Việt |
| --- | --- |
| road traffic crashes | Collisions or accidents on roads |
| vulnerable road users | Road users with less physical protection |
| low- and middle-income countries | Countries with lower national income levels |
| safe system approach | A road safety method that expects human mistakes |
| human error | A mistake made by a person |
| safe speeds | Speed levels that reduce crash danger |
| distracted driving | Driving while attention is on something else |
| reaction times | The time needed to respond |
| traffic signals | Lights or signs that control road movement |
| following distances | Space kept behind another vehicle |
| road infrastructure | Road design, paths, signs and crossings |
| safe crossing points | Places where people can cross roads safely |
| traffic calming measures | Road designs that slow traffic |
| law enforcement | The act of making people obey laws |
| public awareness | What people know about an issue |
| transport planning | Planning roads, stops and transport systems |
| fatal crashes | Crashes that cause death |
| serious injuries | Injuries that are severe |
| accountable | Responsible for actions or decisions |
| community | People who live or work in the same area |
