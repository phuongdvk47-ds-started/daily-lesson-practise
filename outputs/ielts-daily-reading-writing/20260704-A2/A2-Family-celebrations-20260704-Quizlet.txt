unique (/juːˈniːk/) [adj]	Being the only existing one of its type or special - độc đáo, duy nhất. Ví dụ: Every family has a unique way of celebrating.
household (/ˈhaʊs.həʊld/) [noun]	A group of people, often a family, who live together - hộ gia đình. Ví dụ: There are four people in my household.
routines (/ruːˈtiːnz/) [noun]	A usual or fixed way of doing things - thói quen, công việc hàng ngày. Ví dụ: Good routines help children feel safe.
share (/ʃeər/) [verb]	To have or use something at the same time as someone else - chia sẻ. Ví dụ: We share the household chores equally.
arguments (/ˈɑːɡ.jə.mənts/) [noun]	Angry disagreements - sự cãi vã, tranh luận. Ví dụ: They had an argument about who should wash the dishes.
stressful (/ˈstres.fəl/) [adj]	Making you feel worried and nervous - căng thẳng. Ví dụ: Moving to a new house can be very stressful.
events (/ɪˈvents/) [noun]	Things that happen, especially important ones - sự kiện. Ví dụ: A wedding is a very important family event.
important (/ɪmˈpɔː.tənt/) [adj]	Having great value or influence - quan trọng. Ví dụ: It is important to respect older relatives.
gather (/ˈɡæð.ər/) [verb]	To come together in a group - tụ tập, quây quần. Ví dụ: The whole family will gather for Thanksgiving.
prepare (/prɪˈpeər/) [verb]	To make something ready - chuẩn bị. Ví dụ: We prepare special food for the festival.
gifts (/ɡɪfts/) [noun]	Things given to someone without payment - món quà. Ví dụ: Children love receiving gifts on their birthdays.
memories (/ˈmem.ər.iz/) [noun]	Things that you remember from the past - kỷ niệm, ký ức. Ví dụ: We shared many happy memories of our childhood.
bond (/bɒnd/) [noun]	A close connection joining two or more people - sự gắn kết. Ví dụ: The sisters have a very strong bond.
challenges (/ˈtʃæl.ɪndʒɪz/) [noun]	Difficult situations that test someone's ability - thử thách. Ví dụ: Families must face financial challenges together.
share chores (/ʃeər tʃɔːz/) [collocation]	To divide household work among people - chia sẻ việc nhà. Ví dụ: It is fair when everyone shares chores.
keep in touch (/kiːp ɪn tʌtʃ/) [idiom]	To continue to communicate with someone - giữ liên lạc. Ví dụ: I use video calls to keep in touch with my parents.
build a strong bond (/bɪld ə strɒŋ bɒnd/) [collocation]	To create a tight connection between people - xây dựng sự gắn kết bền chặt. Ví dụ: Playing games helps build a strong bond between siblings.
extended family (/ɪkˈsten.dɪd ˈfæm.əl.i/) [noun phrase]	A family unit that includes grandmothers, grandfathers, aunts, and uncles - đại gia đình (gồm nhiều thế hệ). Ví dụ: My extended family always meets at Christmas.
nuclear family (/ˈnjuː.klɪər ˈfæm.əl.i/) [noun phrase]	A family consisting of two parents and their children - gia đình hạt nhân. Ví dụ: A nuclear family usually lives in a smaller house.
relatives (/ˈrel.ə.tɪvz/) [noun]	Members of your family - họ hàng. Ví dụ: All my relatives came to my graduation.
