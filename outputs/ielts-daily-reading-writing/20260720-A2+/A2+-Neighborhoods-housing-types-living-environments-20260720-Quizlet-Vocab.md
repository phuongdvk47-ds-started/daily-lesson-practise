### Phần 1: Học từ vựng đơn giản (Simple Vocabulary)
| Từ vựng tiếng Anh | Nghĩa tiếng Việt |
| --- | --- |
| urban | Connected with a town or city |
| suburban | Connected with an area where people live that is outside the centre of a city |
| rural | Connected with the countryside rather than the town |
| suburbs | The outer parts of a town, where people live |
| settlement | A place where people have come to live and make their homes |
| hamlet | A very small village that does not have its own church |
| facilities | Buildings, services, or equipment that are provided for a particular purpose |
| lifestyle | The way in which a person or a group of people lives |
| transport | A system for carrying people or goods from one place to another |
| advantage | A feature that makes something better than other things |
| disadvantage | A feature that makes something worse than other things |
| crowded | Having a lot of people or too many people |
| distraction | Something that takes your attention away from what you are doing |
| commute | To travel regularly between your home and your place of work |
| working memory | Memory used for a short time |
| musical training | Learning to play music |
| cognitive | Connected with thinking and learning |
| have access to | To have the opportunity to use or see something |
| in contrast | Used to show a clear difference between two things |
| depend on | To need or rely on somebody/something |
