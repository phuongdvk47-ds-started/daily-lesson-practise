### Phần 1: Học từ vựng đơn giản (Simple Vocabulary)
| Từ vựng tiếng Anh | Nghĩa tiếng Việt |
| --- | --- |
| unique | Being the only existing one of its type or special |
| household | A group of people, often a family, who live together |
| routines | A usual or fixed way of doing things |
| share | To have or use something at the same time as someone else |
| arguments | Angry disagreements |
| stressful | Making you feel worried and nervous |
| events | Things that happen, especially important ones |
| important | Having great value or influence |
| gather | To come together in a group |
| prepare | To make something ready |
| gifts | Things given to someone without payment |
| memories | Things that you remember from the past |
| bond | A close connection joining two or more people |
| challenges | Difficult situations that test someone's ability |
| share chores | To divide household work among people |
| keep in touch | To continue to communicate with someone |
| build a strong bond | To create a tight connection between people |
| extended family | A family unit that includes grandmothers, grandfathers, aunts, and uncles |
| nuclear family | A family consisting of two parents and their children |
| relatives | Members of your family |
