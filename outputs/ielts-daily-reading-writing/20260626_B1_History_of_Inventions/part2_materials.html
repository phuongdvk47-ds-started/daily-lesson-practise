I. Vocabulary & Idioms Tables
Table 2.1: Academic Vocabulary (Từ vựng học thuật)
Từ / Cụm từ Phiên âm Loại từ Định nghĩa & Nghĩa tiếng Việt Ví dụ minh họa
equipment /ɪˈkwɪp.mənt/ noun The set of necessary tools or clothing for a purpose.
Trang thiết bị, dụng cụ.		 Most cars have seat belts as part of their equipment.
restraining /rɪˈstreɪ.nɪŋ/ adjective Preventing someone from moving or doing something.
Hạn chế, đai giữ, kiềm chế.		 Safety experts estimate that the restraining devices save lives.
estimate /ˈes.tɪ.meɪt/ verb To guess or calculate the value, size, or amount of something.
Ước lượng, đánh giá.		 Safety experts estimate that seat belts save thousands of lives.
patent /ˈpeɪ.tənt/ noun An official right given by the government to make or sell an invention.
Bằng sáng chế, bằng độc quyền.		 The government gave a patent to Claghorn for his safety belt.
attachment /əˈtætʃ.mənt/ noun An extra part that is joined to something to perform a task.
Phụ kiện, bộ phận gắn thêm.		 The device was said to include hooks and other attachments.
fixed /fɪkst/ adjective Fastened, secure, or not able to be moved.
Cố định, gắn chặt.		 The belt secured the person to a fixed object.
version /ˈvɜː.ʃən/ noun A form of something that is slightly different from other forms.
Phiên bản.		 Other inventors followed with different versions of the seat belt.
abdomen /ˈæb.də.mən/ noun The lower part of the body that contains the stomach.
Vùng bụng, phần bụng.		 At the time, most safety belts in cars crossed the abdomen.
permanent /ˈpɜː.mə.nənt/ adjective Lasting for a long time or forever; not temporary.
Thường trực, lâu dài, vĩnh cửu.		 Volvo was the first to offer the belt as a permanent addition.
addition /əˈdɪʃ.ən/ noun Something that is added to another thing to improve it.
Sự thêm vào, phần bổ sung.		 The seat belt was a valuable addition to modern cars.
honor /ˈɒn.ər/ noun An award or official sign of high respect or praise.
Danh dự, giải thưởng, sự vinh danh.		 The Swedish engineer won many honors for his seat belt.
academy /əˈkæd.ə.mi/ noun An organization to support art, science, or literature.
Học viện, viện hàn lâm.		 He received a gold medal from the Royal Swedish Academy.
car-maker /ˈkɑːˌmeɪ.kər/ noun A company or manufacturer that builds automobiles.
Nhà sản xuất ô tô, hãng xe.		 Volvo shared its seat belt design with other car-makers.
