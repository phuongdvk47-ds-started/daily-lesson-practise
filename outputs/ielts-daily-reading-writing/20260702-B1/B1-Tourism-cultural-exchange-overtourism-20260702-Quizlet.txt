barrier (/ˈbær.i.ər/) [noun]	A fence or obstacle that prevents movement or view - Rào cản, hàng rào. Ví dụ: The town built a black barrier to block Mount Fuji from tourists' view.
overtourism (/ˌəʊ.vəˈtʊə.rɪ.zəm/) [noun]	Too many tourists visiting a place, causing problems - Quá tải du lịch. Ví dụ: Overtourism has made life difficult for residents in many historic cities.
misbehave (/ˌmɪs.bɪˈheɪv/) [verb]	To behave badly or disrespectfully - Cư xử tồi tệ, thiếu lễ độ. Ví dụ: If visitors misbehave, local authorities will set up stricter rules.
trespass (/ˈtres.pəs/) [verb]	To enter someone's land or property without permission - Xâm nhập trái phép. Ví dụ: Tourists should not trespass onto private yards just to take pictures.
viral (/ˈvaɪ.rəl/) [adjective]	Spreading rapidly on the internet - Lan truyền nhanh, gây bão mạng. Ví dụ: The photo of Mount Fuji sitting on top of the convenience store went viral.
sensation (/senˈseɪ.ʃən/) [noun]	Something very exciting or interesting to the public - Hiện tượng, sự giật gân. Ví dụ: The Lawson shop became a viral sensation on social media within days.
overreaction (/ˌəʊ.və.riˈæk.ʃən/) [noun]	Reacting too strongly or with too much force - Sự phản ứng thái quá. Ví dụ: Some visitors think the black barrier is an overreaction by town officials.
manners (/ˈmæn.əz/) [noun]	Ways of behaving towards others; polite behavior - Cách cư xử, phép tắc. Ví dụ: Travelers should remember their manners and respect local customs.
restrictions (/rɪˈstrɪk.ʃənz/) [noun]	Official rules that limit what people can do - Các giới hạn, lệnh cấm. Ví dụ: Tourist numbers increased rapidly after pandemic travel restrictions were lifted.
background (/ˈbæk.ɡraʊnd/) [noun]	The part of a picture or view behind the main subject - Hậu cảnh, nền phía sau. Ví dụ: Mount Fuji was a beautiful background in the viral photograph.
crowd (/kraʊd/) [noun/verb]	A large group of people; to gather in large numbers - Đám đông, tụ tập đông đúc. Ví dụ: The city built a screen to keep out the crowds of tourists.
security (/sɪˈkjʊə.rə.ti/) [noun]	Protection against danger, noise, or bad behavior - Sự bảo an, an ninh. Ví dụ: The town hired security guards to help control the visitors.
ramp up (/ræmp ʌp/) [verb]	To increase or grow significantly - Tăng tốc, tăng trưởng mạnh. Ví dụ: Tourism has really ramped up in Japan over the past two years.
convenience store (/kənˈviː.ni.əns stɔːr/) [noun]	A small shop that is open for long hours, selling food - Cửa hàng tiện lợi. Ví dụ: The convenience store was crowded with tourists taking photos.
resident (/ˈrez.ɪ.dənt/) [noun]	A person who lives in a particular place - Cư dân địa phương. Ví dụ: Local residents complained about loud noises early in the morning.
landmark (/ˈlænd.mɑːk/) [noun]	A building or place that is easily recognized - Địa danh nổi tiếng, cột mốc. Ví dụ: Mount Fuji is one of the most famous landmarks in Japan.
worsen (/ˈwɜː.sən/) [verb]	To become worse than before - Trở nên tồi tệ hơn. Ví dụ: Noise and traffic problems have worsened in recent months.
destination (/ˌdes.tɪˈneɪ.ʃən/) [noun]	The place where someone is going - Điểm đến du lịch. Ví dụ: Japan has become a highly popular travel destination for families.
