houseboat (/ˈhaʊs.bəʊt/) [noun]	A boat that people live in - Nhà thuyền, nhà nổi. Ví dụ: They live on a small houseboat at the Cai Rang floating market.
unrealistic (/ˌʌn.rɪəˈlɪs.tɪk/) [adjective]	Not showing a clear understanding of what is possible - Phi thực tế. Ví dụ: He knew his dream of becoming a singer was unrealistic.
livelihood (/ˈlaɪv.li.hʊd/) [noun]	A means of securing the necessities of life - Sinh kế, cách kiếm sống. Ví dụ: Farming is the main livelihood for many residents in the delta.
drought (/draʊt/) [noun]	A long period of abnormally low rainfall, leading to a shortage of water - Hạn hán. Ví dụ: The dry season brought a severe drought, ruining the rice crops.
dam (/dæm/) [noun]	A barrier built to hold back water and raise its level - Đập thủy điện/đập ngăn nước. Ví dụ: Upstream dams have reduced the flow of fresh water to the delta.
poverty (/ˈhɒv.ə.ti/) [noun]	The state of being extremely poor - Cảnh nghèo đói. Ví dụ: Many families move to big cities to escape extreme poverty.
guarantee (/ˌɡær.ənˈtiː/) [verb/noun]	To promise that something will be done or will happen - Bảo đảm, cam kết. Ví dụ: Moving to Ho Chi Minh City does not guarantee a high salary.
hardship (/ˈhɑːd.ʃɪp/) [noun]	Severe suffering or privation - Sự gian khổ, khó khăn. Ví dụ: The grandmother faced many hardships raising the twins on a houseboat.
modernize (/ˈmɒd.ən.aɪz/) [verb]	To adapt something to modern needs or habits - Hiện đại hóa. Ví dụ: The government approved a plan to modernize the local agricultural economy.
investment (/ɪnˈvest.mənt/) [noun]	The action or process of investing money for profit or material result - Sự đầu tư. Ví dụ: New highways will attract foreign investment to the countryside.
resist (/rɪˈzɪst/) [verb]	To withstand the action or effect of - Kháng cự, cưỡng lại. Ví dụ: The draw of Ho Chi Minh City is very hard to resist for young people.
cooperate (/kəʊˈɒp.ər.eɪt/) [verb]	To work together for a common purpose - Hợp tác. Ví dụ: Neighbors cooperate with each other to repair their houseboats.
severe (/sɪˈvɪər/) [adjective]	Very great; intense; harsh - Nghiêm trọng, khốc liệt. Ví dụ: The Mekong Delta faces severe environmental issues due to climate change.
consequently (/ˈhɒn.sɪ.kwənt.li/) [adverb]	As a result - Kết quả là, do đó. Ví dụ: Crop yields fell; consequently, many farmers lost their land.
migrate (/maɪˈɡreɪt/) [verb]	To move from one region or habitat to another - Di cư. Ví dụ: Millions of rural workers migrate to cities in search of factory jobs.
upstream (/ʌpˈstriːm/) [adjective/adverb]	Moving or situated opposite to the direction of a stream - Thượng nguồn. Ví dụ: Upstream dams in neighboring countries have changed the river's flow.
decline (/dɪˈklaɪn/) [noun/verb]	A gradual and continuous loss of strength, numbers, or value - Sự suy giảm. Ví dụ: The economic decline forced many businesses to shut down.
