| vegetable | A plant used as food |
| nutritious | Containing many useful nutrients |
| nutrient | A substance that helps plants or animals grow |
| boil | To cook in boiling water |
| steam | To cook food over boiling water |
| microwave | To cook using waves of energy |
| leach | To dissolve and escape into liquid |
| repurpose | To use for a new purpose |
| blanch | To cook vegetables in boiling water briefly |
| absorb | To take in liquid, gas, or nutrients |
| antioxidant | Substance that protects cells |
| digest | To break down food in the stomach |
| dietitian | A person who studies food and health |
| maximize | To increase to the greatest amount |
| soluble | Able to be dissolved in a liquid |
| preserve | To keep something safe or fresh |
| method | A particular way of doing something |
| benefit | An advantage or useful effect |
| recommend | To advise someone to do something |
