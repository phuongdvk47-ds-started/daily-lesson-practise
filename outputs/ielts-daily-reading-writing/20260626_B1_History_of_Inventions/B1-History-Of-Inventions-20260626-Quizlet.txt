equipment (/ɪˈkwɪp.mənt/) [n]	The set of necessary tools or clothing for a purpose - Trang thiết bị, dụng cụ. Ví dụ: Most cars have seat belts as part of their equipment.
restraining (/rɪˈstreɪ.nɪŋ/) [adj]	Preventing someone or something from moving freely - Hạn chế, đai giữ. Ví dụ: Safety experts estimate that the restraining devices save lives.
estimate (/ˈes.tɪ.meɪt/) [v]	To roughly calculate or judge the value, number, or amount of something - Ước tính, ước lượng. Ví dụ: Scientists estimate that seat belts save thousands of lives each year.
patent (/ˈpeɪ.tənt/) [n]	An official document giving the inventor the right to make or sell an invention - Bằng sáng chế. Ví dụ: The government gave a patent to Edward Claghorn to protect his invention.
attachment (/əˈtætʃ.mənt/) [n]	A device or part that is joined to something else - Phụ kiện gắn liền, vật đính kèm. Ví dụ: The belt included hooks and other attachments for securing the person.
fixed (/fɪkst/) [adj]	Fastened so that it cannot be moved or changed - Cố định, gắn chặt. Ví dụ: The Safety-Belt was attached to a fixed object inside the car.
version (/ˈvɜː.ʒən/) [n]	A form of something that differs from other forms - Phiên bản, dạng biến thể. Ví dụ: Other inventors created different versions of the original seat belt.
abdomen (/ˈæb.də.mən/) [n]	The part of the body between the chest and the hips - Bụng, vùng bụng. Ví dụ: Early seat belts crossed the body over the abdomen.
permanent (/ˈpɜː.mə.nənt/) [adj]	Lasting for a very long time or forever; not temporary - Lâu dài, vĩnh viễn. Ví dụ: Volvo offered the seat belt as a permanent addition to its cars.
addition (/əˈdɪʃ.ən/) [n]	Something added to improve or complete something else - Sự thêm vào, phần bổ sung. Ví dụ: The seat belt was a valuable addition to modern car safety.
honor (/ˈɒn.ər/) [n]	An award or official sign of high respect or praise - Danh dự, giải thưởng. Ví dụ: The Swedish engineer won many honors for his seat belt invention.
academy (/əˈkæd.ə.mi/) [n]	An organization to support art, science, or literature - Học viện, viện hàn lâm. Ví dụ: He received a medal from the Royal Swedish Academy of Engineering Sciences.
car-maker (/ˈkɑːˌmeɪ.kər/) [n]	A company or manufacturer that builds automobiles - Nhà sản xuất ô tô. Ví dụ: Volvo shared its seat belt design with other car-makers.
seat belt (/ˈsiːt ˌbelt/) [n]	A safety belt used in a car or plane to hold a passenger securely - Dây đai an toàn. Ví dụ: You should always fasten your seat belt before driving.
three-point (/θriː pɔɪnt/) [adj phrase]	A belt design anchored at three points for safety - Đai ba điểm. Ví dụ: Bohlin invented the three-point seat belt which is still used in all cars today.
safety expert (/ˈseɪf.ti ˌek.spɜːt/) [n]	A professional who studies and makes recommendations on safety - Chuyên gia an toàn. Ví dụ: Safety experts said the new design was far more effective.
flying machine (/ˈflaɪ.ɪŋ məˌʃiːn/) [n]	An early type of aircraft before modern aviation was developed - Cỗ máy bay thời sơ khai. Ví dụ: George Cayley is remembered for his early flying machines.
in case of (/ɪn keɪs əv/) [phrase]	In the event of a particular situation, usually an emergency - Trong trường hợp, phòng khi. Ví dụ: Seat belts protect passengers in case of accident.
lap and shoulder (/læp ænd ˈʃəʊl.dər/) [phrase]	Crossing both the hips and chest for maximum protection - Dây đai qua đùi và vai. Ví dụ: A lap and shoulder belt is safer than a simple lap belt.
win honors (/wɪn ˈɒn.əz/) [phrase]	To receive awards or recognition for achievements - Được vinh danh, đạt giải thưởng. Ví dụ: The Swedish engineer won honors all over the world.
