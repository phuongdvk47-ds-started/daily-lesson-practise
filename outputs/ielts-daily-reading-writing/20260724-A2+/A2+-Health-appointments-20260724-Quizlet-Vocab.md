### Phần 1: Học từ vựng đơn giản (Simple Vocabulary)
| Từ vựng tiếng Anh | Nghĩa tiếng Việt |
| --- | --- |
| appointment | A formal arrangement to meet or visit someone at a particular time |
| receptionist | A person whose job is to deal with people arriving at a clinic or office |
| symptom | A physical or mental feature indicating an illness or disease |
| consultation | A meeting with a doctor to seek advice or treatment |
| prescription | An official paper from a doctor authorizing medicine |
| clinic | A building where people receive medical advice or treatment |
| healthcare professional | A qualified person who provides health services like doctors or nurses |
| medical history | A record of a patient's past health conditions and treatments |
| health checkup | A thorough general medical examination |
| treatment plan | A detailed outline of medical care decided by a doctor |
| urgent care | Immediate medical care for non-life-threatening illnesses |
| follow-up visit | A return visit to check progress after medical treatment |
| patient | A person receiving medical care or treatment |
| surgery | A place where a GP doctor sees patients in the UK |
| registration form | An official document filled in to join a clinic |
| blood test | A scientific examination of a sample of blood |
| examine | To inspect a patient carefully to check health |
| advise | To offer medical suggestions or recommendations |
| pharmacist | A professional qualified to prepare and sell drugs |
| make an appointment | To schedule a visit with a doctor |
