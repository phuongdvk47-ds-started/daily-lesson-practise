| Từ vựng tiếng Anh | Nghĩa tiếng Việt |
| --- | --- |
| cardiovascular | Relating to the heart and blood vessels |
| disengagement | The state of being uninvolved or detached from something |
| self-discipline | The ability to make yourself do things you should do |
| sportsmanship | Fair and generous behavior or treatment of others in a sports contest |
| socialization | The process of learning to behave in a way that is acceptable to society |
| undermine | To weaken or ruin something gradually |
| destructive | Causing severe damage or harm |
| propensity | A natural tendency to behave in a particular way |
| referee | A person who controls a sports match and makes decisions |
| transferable | Able to be used or applied in a different situation |
| play a role | To have an influence or be involved in something |
| win at all costs | To do anything necessary to achieve victory, even if it is dishonest |
| team player | A person who works well as a member of a group or team |
| cooperative | Willing to help or work with others |
| fair play | Respect for the rules and equal opportunities in sports |
| combat | To try to stop or destroy something harmful |
| peer pressure | Pressure from people of your own age to behave like them |
| mutual trust | Trust that two or more people have in each other |
| sustain a streak | To keep a continuous series of success going |
| common goal | An objective shared by all members of a group |
