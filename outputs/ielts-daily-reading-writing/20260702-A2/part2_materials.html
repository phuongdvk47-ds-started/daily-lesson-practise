II. Grammar Detail & Strategy (Lý thuyết & Mẹo)
Chủ điểm 1: Comparatives, Superlatives and Modifiers
  • So sánh hơn (Comparatives): Dùng tính từ ngắn + er hoặc more + tính từ dài + than.
  • Ví dụ: longer than, more popular than.
  • Từ bổ nghĩa so sánh (Modifiers): Dùng để nhấn mạnh mức độ khác biệt.
  • significantly / much (nhiều, đáng kể): "The year-round system is significantly longer than the traditional one."
  • slightly / a little (một chút, nhẹ): "Adding days is slightly more expensive."
  • twice as ... as (gấp hai lần): "School in Japan is twice as long as in other countries."
Chủ điểm 2: Future with 'Will' and 'Going to'
  • Will + V-inf: Dùng cho dự đoán tương lai hoặc quyết định tức thời. "The schedule will change next year."
  • Be going to + V-inf: Dùng cho kế hoạch, dự định đã có từ trước. "The school board is going to discuss the calendar tomorrow."
Chủ điểm 3: Tips & Tricks: Modals of Obligation and Advice
  • Must / Have to + V-inf: Diễn tả sự bắt buộc, bắt buộc do luật pháp. "Students have to attend 180 days."
  • Should + V-inf: Diễn tả lời khuyên hữu ích. "Schools should provide air conditioning."
  • Chú ý: Không dùng 'to' sau should hoặc must.
III. Common Mistakes / IELTS Traps for This Topic
Common mistake / Trap Wrong example Correct version Why it matters for IELTS
Double Comparatives The school day is more longer now. The school day is longer now. IELTS writing checks for basic grammatical accuracy. Do not combine 'more' with short adjectives that already end in '-er'.
Incorrect Modal Infinitive You must to arrive early. You must arrive early. The modal 'must' is followed directly by the base verb without 'to'.
Ratio Comparison preposition error Year-round is twice as expensive than traditional. Year-round is twice as expensive as traditional. The double comparative ratio structure always requires 'twice as ... as', not 'than'.
