### Phần 1: Học từ vựng đơn giản (Simple Vocabulary)
| Từ vựng tiếng Anh | Nghĩa tiếng Việt |
| --- | --- |
| endangered | Animals or plants that may soon not exist. |
| extinct | No longer existing. |
| habitat | The natural home of an animal or plant. |
| protect | To keep someone or something safe. |
| species | A set of animals or plants in which the members have similar characteristics. |
| recovery | The process of returning to a normal or healthy state. |
| wildlife | Animals, birds, and plants that live in natural conditions. |
| breed | To produce young animals. |
| population | All the organisms of the same group or species in an area. |
| prey | An animal that is hunted and killed by another for food. |
| threat | Something that is likely to cause danger or harm. |
| successful | Achieving the results that you wanted. |
| decrease | To become less, or to make something less. |
| increase | To become larger or greater in amount. |
| prevent | To stop something from happening. |
| preserve | To keep something safe or in its original state. |
| decline | A continuous decrease in number or quality. |
| captive | Kept in a space and not free to leave. |
| release | To allow an animal to run free in the wild. |
| survive | To continue to live or exist after danger. |
| wildlife-conservation | The practice of protecting wild species and their habitats. |
| well-known | Known widely or thoroughly; famous. |
| plant-eating | Feeding on plants (herbivorous). |
| captive-bred | Animals born and raised in controlled environments. |
| grassland | Large open area of country covered with grass. |
| die out | To become less common and finally stop existing completely. |
| run out of | To use all of something and have no more left. |
