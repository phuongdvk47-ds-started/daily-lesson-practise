### Phần 1: Học từ vựng đơn giản (Simple Vocabulary)
| Từ vựng tiếng Anh | Nghĩa tiếng Việt |
| --- | --- |
| equipment | The set of necessary tools or clothing for a purpose |
| restraining | Preventing someone or something from moving freely |
| estimate | To roughly calculate or judge the value, number, or amount of something |
| patent | An official document giving the inventor the right to make or sell an invention |
| attachment | A device or part that is joined to something else |
| fixed | Fastened so that it cannot be moved or changed |
| version | A form of something that differs from other forms |
| abdomen | The part of the body between the chest and the hips |
| permanent | Lasting for a very long time or forever; not temporary |
| addition | Something added to improve or complete something else |
| honor | An award or official sign of high respect or praise |
| academy | An organization to support art, science, or literature |
| car-maker | A company or manufacturer that builds automobiles |
| seat belt | A safety belt used in a car or plane to hold a passenger securely |
| three-point | A belt design anchored at three points for safety |
| safety expert | A professional who studies and makes recommendations on safety |
| flying machine | An early type of aircraft before modern aviation was developed |
| in case of | In the event of a particular situation, usually an emergency |
| lap and shoulder | Crossing both the hips and chest for maximum protection |
| win honors | To receive awards or recognition for achievements |
