appointment (/əˈpɔɪnt.mənt/) [noun]	A formal arrangement to meet or visit somebody at a particular time, especially for medical care - cuộc hẹn, lịch hẹn (đặc biệt là với bác sĩ). Ví dụ: I have a doctor's appointment at 3 p.m. tomorrow.
symptom (/ˈsɪmp.təm/) [noun]	A physical feeling or change that shows you have an illness - triệu chứng (bệnh). Ví dụ: A cough and a fever are common symptoms of a cold.
pharmacist (/ˈfɑː.mə.sɪst/) [noun]	A person who is trained to prepare and sell medicines - dược sĩ. Ví dụ: The pharmacist gave me medicine for my headache.
receptionist (/rɪˈsep.ʃən.ɪst/) [noun]	A person who works at the front desk and welcomes visitors or patients - nhân viên lễ tân, nhân viên tiếp đón. Ví dụ: The receptionist asked me to wait in the waiting room.
online (/ˌɒnˈlaɪn/) [adjective / adverb]	Connected to or available through the internet - trực tuyến, qua mạng. Ví dụ: You can book your appointment online.
patient (/ˈpeɪ.ʃənt/) [noun]	A person who is receiving medical care or treatment - bệnh nhân. Ví dụ: The doctor saw twenty patients today.
treatment (/ˈtriːt.mənt/) [noun]	Medical care given to a patient for an illness or injury - sự điều trị, cách chữa trị. Ví dụ: She received treatment for her back pain.
urgent (/ˈɜː.dʒənt/) [adjective]	Needing immediate action or attention - khẩn cấp, cấp bách. Ví dụ: If your problem is urgent, call the doctor right away.
register (/ˈredʒ.ɪ.stər/) [verb]	To put your name on an official list, especially at a doctor's surgery - đăng ký, ghi danh. Ví dụ: You need to register with a GP surgery before making an appointment.
vaccination (/ˌvæk.sɪˈneɪ.ʃən/) [noun]	An injection that protects a person from a disease - sự tiêm chủng, tiêm vắc-xin. Ví dụ: Children need a vaccination before starting school.
health centre (/helθ ˈsen.tər/) [noun phrase]	A building where several doctors have offices and medical services are provided - trung tâm y tế. Ví dụ: There is a new health centre near the train station.
medical history (/ˈmed.ɪ.kəl ˈhɪs.tər.i/) [noun phrase]	A record of information about a person's past health and illnesses - tiền sử bệnh án, lịch sử y tế. Ví dụ: The doctor wrote down my medical history.
physical examination (/ˈfɪz.ɪ.kəl ɪɡˌzæm.ɪˈneɪ.ʃən/) [noun phrase]	A check of a person's body by a doctor to make sure they are healthy - khám sức khỏe tổng quát. Ví dụ: The doctor did a physical examination to check my heart and lungs.
health condition (/helθ kənˈdɪʃ.ən/) [noun phrase]	The state of a person's health, especially when they have a medical problem - tình trạng sức khỏe. Ví dụ: The doctor asked about my health condition.
make an appointment (/meɪk ən əˈpɔɪnt.mənt/) [verb phrase]	To arrange a time to see a doctor or other professional - đặt lịch hẹn. Ví dụ: I need to make an appointment with the dentist.
blood pressure (/blʌd ˈpreʃ.ər/) [noun phrase]	The force with which blood flows through the body, measured by a doctor - huyết áp. Ví dụ: The nurse checked my blood pressure before the doctor came in.
waiting room (/ˈweɪ.tɪŋ ˌruːm/) [noun phrase]	A room where people sit and wait, especially before seeing a doctor - phòng chờ. Ví dụ: There were five other people in the waiting room.
common illness (/ˈkɒm.ən ˈɪl.nəs/) [noun phrase]	A health problem that many people often experience, like a cold or flu - bệnh thông thường. Ví dụ: Colds and flu are common illnesses in winter.
feel better (/fiːl ˈbet.ər/) [verb phrase]	To become healthy again or to be in less pain after being sick - cảm thấy khỏe hơn. Ví dụ: I hope you feel better soon after taking the medicine.
check-up (/ˈtʃek.ʌp/) [noun]	A general medical examination to make sure someone is healthy - khám sức khỏe định kỳ, kiểm tra sức khỏe. Ví dụ: I go to the doctor for a check-up once a year.
