habit (/ˈhæb.ɪt/) [noun]	A thing that you do often and almost without thinking - thói quen. Ví dụ: Reviewing your schedule daily is a very good habit.
routine (/ruːˈtiːn/) [noun]	The normal order and way in which you regularly do things - thói quen hằng ngày, quy trình. Ví dụ: A morning routine helps you start the day calmly.
schedule (/ˈʃedʒ.uːl/) [noun]	A plan that lists all the work that you have to do - lịch trình, thời khóa biểu. Ví dụ: I always write my exam dates in my study schedule.
productivity (/ˌprɒd.ʌkˈtɪv.ə.ti/) [noun]	The rate at which a person does useful work - năng suất, hiệu quả làm việc. Ví dụ: Planning tasks in advance can boost your daily productivity.
focus (/ˈfəʊ.kəs/) [noun]	The concentration of attention on a particular activity - sự tập trung. Ví dụ: Quiet libraries allow students to study with great focus.
active (/ˈæk.tɪv/) [adjective]	Doing things that require physical effort and energy - tích cực, năng động. Ví dụ: Playing sports keeps high school students active and healthy.
alert (/əˈlɜːt/) [adjective]	Able to think quickly and notice things - tỉnh táo, nhanh nhạy. Ví dụ: A short walk in the morning makes you feel more alert.
lifestyle (/ˈlaɪf.staɪl/) [noun]	The way in which a person lives - lối sống, phong cách sống. Ví dụ: They want to change their lifestyle and live in a calmer environment.
commute (/kəˈmjuːt/) [verb]	To travel regularly between home and work or school - đi lại đều đặn hàng ngày. Ví dụ: Many students commute to school by bicycle.
healthy (/ˈhel.θi/) [adjective]	Helping to produce good health - khỏe mạnh, lành mạnh. Ví dụ: Eating a balanced breakfast is a healthy morning habit.
timetable (/ˈtaɪm.teɪ.bl̩/) [noun]	A list showing the times at which events will happen - thời khóa biểu. Ví dụ: We received our new class timetable last week.
prioritize (/praɪˈɒr.ɪ.taɪz/) [verb]	To treat something as being more important than other things - ưu tiên. Ví dụ: You should prioritize your homework before playing games.
wellbeing (/ˌwelˈbiː.ɪŋ/) [noun]	The state of being comfortable, healthy, or happy - sự khỏe mạnh, hạnh phúc. Ví dụ: Getting enough sleep is vital for a student's mental wellbeing.
distraction (/dɪˈstræk.ʃən/) [noun]	Something that takes your attention away from study - sự xao nhãng, làm mất tập trung. Ví dụ: Put your phone away to avoid any distraction.
facilities (/fəˈsɪl.ə.tiz/) [noun plural]	Buildings, services, or equipment provided for a purpose - cơ sở vật chất, tiện ích. Ví dụ: The school facilities include a library and science labs.
working memory (/ˈwɜː.kɪŋ ˈmem.ər.i/) [noun phrase]	Memory used to hold information temporarily - trí nhớ làm việc. Ví dụ: Working memory is key to solving math equations.
musical training (/ˈmjuː.zɪ.kəl ˈtreɪ.nɪŋ/) [noun phrase]	Learning to play music or an instrument - sự luyện tập âm nhạc. Ví dụ: Musical training helps develop children's focus.
cognitive (/ˈkɒɡ.nə.tɪv/) [adjective]	Connected with mental processes of learning - thuộc về nhận thức. Ví dụ: Cognitive development speeds up during childhood.
time management (/ˈtaɪm ˈmæn.ɪdʒ.mənt/) [noun phrase]	The ability to use one's time effectively - sự quản lý thời gian. Ví dụ: Time management is an essential skill for successful studying.
on track (/ɒn træk/) [phrase]	Progressing in the way that was planned - đúng lịch trình, đi đúng hướng. Ví dụ: Keep your goals clear to make sure you stay on track.
