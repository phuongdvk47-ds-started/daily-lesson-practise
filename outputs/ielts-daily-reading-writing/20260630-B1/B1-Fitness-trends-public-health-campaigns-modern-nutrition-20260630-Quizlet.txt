resolution (/ˌrez.əˈluː.ʃən/) [noun]	A promise to yourself to do something - Quyết tâm, cam kết với bản thân. Ví dụ: Her New Year's resolution was to walk ten thousand steps every day.
structured (/ˈstrʌk.tʃəd/) [adjective]	Organized and planned in a clean way - Có kế hoạch, có cấu trúc rõ ràng. Ví dụ: Running in a park is great, but some people prefer structured fitness classes.
sedentary (/ˈsed.ən.tər.i/) [adjective]	Spending a lot of time sitting down - Ít vận động, ngồi nhiều. Ví dụ: Office workers often lead a sedentary lifestyle and need to walk more.
moderate (/ˈmɒd.ər.ət/) [adjective]	Neither too much nor too little - Vừa phải, trung bình. Ví dụ: Brisk walking is an example of moderate exercise that improves health.
intense (/ɪnˈtens/) [adjective]	Requiring great energy or effort - Cường độ cao, mạnh mẽ. Ví dụ: High-intensity interval training involves short, intense bursts of exercise.
cardiovascular (/ˌkɑː.di.əʊˈvæs.kjə.lər/) [adjective]	Relating to the heart and blood vessels - Thuộc về tim mạch. Ví dụ: Regular cycling is excellent for strengthening your cardiovascular system.
benefit (/ˈben.ɪ.fɪt/) [noun/verb]	An advantage; to be useful to someone - Lợi ích, mang lại lợi ích. Ví dụ: Walking in the park will benefit both your physical and mental health.
guideline (/ˈɡaɪd.laɪn/) [noun]	Official advice or rules on how to do something - Hướng dẫn, nguyên tắc chỉ đạo. Ví dụ: The government has issued new guidelines on exercise and nutrition.
incorporate (/ɪnˈkɔː.pər.eɪt/) [verb]	To include something as part of something larger - Kết hợp, lồng ghép. Ví dụ: Try to incorporate small movements, like stretching, into your daily routine.
strategy (/ˈstræt.ə.dʒi/) [noun]	A detailed plan for achieving a success - Chiến lược, phương pháp. Ví dụ: Habit stacking is a useful strategy for building long-term fitness habits.
prevent (/prɪˈvent/) [verb]	To stop something from happening - Ngăn ngừa, phòng tránh. Ví dụ: Regular physical activity can help to prevent many heart conditions.
lifestyle (/ˈlaɪf.staɪl/) [noun]	The way a person lives - Lối sống, phong cách sống. Ví dụ: Changing to an active lifestyle is the best way to stay fit.
duration (/dʒʊəˈreɪ.ʃən/) [noun]	The length of time that something lasts - Khoảng thời gian kéo dài. Ví dụ: The duration of the workout should be at least ten minutes per session.
consistent (/kənˈsɪs.tənt/) [adjective]	Always behaving or happening in a similar, positive way - Nhất quán, kiên trì. Ví dụ: Being consistent with exercise is much more important than workout length.
challenge (/ˈtʃæl.ɪndʒ/) [noun/verb]	Something that needs great mental or physical effort - Thử thách. Ví dụ: Finding thirty minutes to exercise every single day can be a big challenge.
motivation (/ˌməʊ.tɪˈveɪ.ʃən/) [noun]	The enthusiasm or reason for doing something - Động lực. Ví dụ: Music can provide the motivation you need to finish a difficult run.
recommended (/ˌrek.əˈmen.dɪd/) [adjective]	Suggested as a good options or choice - Được khuyến nghị, khuyên dùng. Ví dụ: Walking is a highly recommended activity for people of all ages.
target (/ˈtɑː.ɡɪt/) [noun/verb]	An object or result that you try to achieve - Mục tiêu, hướng tới. Ví dụ: She managed to reach her daily target of ten thousand steps.
physical (/ˈfɪz.ɪ.kəl/) [adjective]	Relating to the body rather than the mind - Thuộc thể chất. Ví dụ: Swimming provides a full physical workout for the entire body.
statistics (/stəˈtɪs.tɪks/) [noun]	A collection of numerical facts or data - Số liệu thống kê. Ví dụ: Official statistics show that active people live longer, healthier lives.
