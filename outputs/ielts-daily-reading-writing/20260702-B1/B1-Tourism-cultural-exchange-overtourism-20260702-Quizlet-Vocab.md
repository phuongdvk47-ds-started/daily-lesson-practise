### Phần 1: Học từ vựng đơn giản (Simple Vocabulary)
| Từ vựng tiếng Anh | Nghĩa tiếng Việt |
| --- | --- |
| barrier | A fence or obstacle that prevents movement or view |
| overtourism | Too many tourists visiting a place, causing problems |
| misbehave | To behave badly or disrespectfully |
| trespass | To enter someone's land or property without permission |
| viral | Spreading rapidly on the internet |
| sensation | Something very exciting or interesting to the public |
| overreaction | Reacting too strongly or with too much force |
| manners | Ways of behaving towards others; polite behavior |
| restrictions | Official rules that limit what people can do |
| background | The part of a picture or view behind the main subject |
| crowd | A large group of people; to gather in large numbers |
| security | Protection against danger, noise, or bad behavior |
| ramp up | To increase or grow significantly |
| convenience store | A small shop that is open for long hours, selling food |
| resident | A person who lives in a particular place |
| landmark | A building or place that is easily recognized |
| worsen | To become worse than before |
| destination | The place where someone is going |
