### Phần 1: Học từ vựng đơn giản (Simple Vocabulary)
| Từ vựng tiếng Anh | Nghĩa tiếng Việt |
| --- | --- |
| accountable | Responsible for decisions or actions |
| nuisance | Something that causes annoyance or trouble |
| unprecedented | Never having happened or existed before |
| amplify | To increase the strength or effect of something |
| liability | Legal responsibility for something |
| algorithm | A set of rules or steps followed by a computer |
| nuance | A very slight difference in appearance, meaning, or sound |
| suicide | The act of intentionally killing oneself |
| lawsuit | A problem taken to a law court by an ordinary person or organization |
| recommend | To suggest that someone or something would be good |
| sadness | The feeling of being unhappy |
| promote | To encourage something to happen or develop |
| advocate | To publicly support or suggest an idea or plan |
| settlement | An official agreement that finishes a disagreement |
| professional | A person who has a job that needs special training |
| crisis | A time of great disagreement, confusion, or suffering |
| mental health | The condition of someone's mind and active feelings |
