I. Reading Passage
The Benefits of Hobbies and Sports Clubs

Hobbies are regular activities that people do for enjoyment, usually during their leisure time. There are many different hobbies available for people of all ages. For example, some people like playing outdoor sports, while others prefer quiet indoor hobbies like reading, drawing, or playing chess. Joining a local club is a great way to meet new friends who share the same interests. In a sports club, members can practice together and improve their skills.

Participating in hobbies helps individuals learn important skills and gain knowledge. When you start a new activity, you often need to book a class or buy some special equipment. For instance, if you want to play tennis, you must find a court and buy a racket. Many local schools and community centers have sports clubs where people can register easily. These activities help people relax and reduce stress after work.

If you want to join a sports club, you can make a request at the office or online. Members can choose different times for their weekly activities because clubs are open every day. Hobbies make our daily lives more interesting and help us stay active. Whether you like creative arts or energetic sports, there is always a perfect hobby that you can enjoy.
